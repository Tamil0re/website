About GWC USM

Girls Who Code at The University of Southern Mississippi is an organization dedicated to empowering students to excel in the technology field through skill-building workshops, collaborative projects, and networking opportunities. At GWC USM, we embrace the principles of diversity and innovation. Our approach is guided by the pillars of Ask - Learn - Teach - Impact, fostering a collaborative and inclusive environment. Our mission is to provide enriching and inclusive space where students from all backgrounds can grow their skills, build knowledge, and connect with the broader tech community. We welcome all students regardless of gender identity who are passionate about coding, innovation, and collaboration.

Community

Building a supportive network of women in tech who lift each other up and create lasting friendships.

Education

Providing hands-on learning opportunities through workshops, hackathons, and coding challenges.

Empowerment

Inspiring confidence and leadership skills to help members excel in their future tech careers.

Our Mission

"To inspire, educate, and equip students with the computing skills to pursue 21st-century opportunities and to use technology to solve problems that matter to them, their families, their communities, and the world."

– Girls Who Code
